Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly to us when you make a booking or contact us, including your name, phone number, email address, and service preferences.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to process bookings, communicate appointment details, and improve our services. We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your service request.

Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through Paystack. We do not store credit card or banking information on our servers.

Data Storage

Booking data is stored locally for availability management. Completed booking records may be stored to maintain appointment history.
